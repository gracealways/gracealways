Students of Grace Always Preparatory School, Accra come from Ghana, the West African sub-region, and all over the world. The dual qualification policy of the School makes it the preferred choice of most prospective students. Every student who enrolled to the school register for a corresponding professional programme of the student’s choice and receives free tuition. This makes it possible for most students who graduate with both primary and the junior high school receives professional certificates etc.

Grace Always Preparatory School, Accra invites applications from qualified applicants for admission to its programmes for the 2022/2023 academic year.

Every student who enrolled to the school is offered the opportunity to register for a corresponding professional programme of the student’s choice and receives free tuition and certificate such as the BECE etc.

Earn a good grade to enter into any High School of your choice. Applications are invited from qualified applicants for admission to the under-listed primary Programmes, offered in blended mode with more than 70% online learning interactions and activities, for the 2022/2023 academic year.

The School makes every effort to provide financial assistance to brilliant but needy qualified Ghanaian undergraduate applicants through the headmaster Endowment Fund. The School acting through the Centre for International Education and Collaboration welcomes International Students by applying for direct enrolment to our programmes or through exchange programmes.
